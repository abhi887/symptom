About Us

The Sympchec Medical Team works closely with a team of over 100 nationwide doctors and health experts across a broad range of specialty areas to ensure Sympchec's content is up to date, accurate, and helps you live a healthier life.

What We Do For Our Users

Sympchec has created an organization that we believe fulfills the promise of health information on the Internet. And that, we believe, requires dedicated, full-time staff professionals with state-of-the-art expertise in:
-Health news for the public
-Creating and maintaining up-to-date medical reference content  databases
-User experience
-Interactive tools
As serious as we are about credibility, we also know that at times, health information can and should be engaging, exciting, and entertaining. We pride ourselves in knowing our audience's needs and delivering the most appropriate experience. Our mission is to fulfill all these needs in the most appropriate ways possible. We are committed to improving our site.
